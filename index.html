About Our Kennel

A Legacy of Excellence

For over three decades, Amazing Creatures Kennel has been synonymous with excellence in breeding and raising premium poodles. Our commitment to health, temperament, and breed standards has made us a trusted name among discerning dog enthusiasts worldwide.

Every poodle in our care receives individual attention, premium nutrition, and early socialization to ensure they become well-adjusted, intelligent companions.

Our kennel is FCI registered (FCI 7364) and follows the highest ethical breeding practices. We specialize in Standard, Miniature, and Toy poodles in a variety of colors and coat types.

Our Poodle Varieties

Standard Poodle

The elegant and athletic Standard Poodle stands over 15 inches tall. Known for their intelligence and versatility, they excel in various dog sports and make wonderful family companions.

15-24 inches 45-70 lbs Available

Miniature Poodle

Perfect for those who want a compact but active companion. Miniature Poodles stand 10-15 inches tall and possess all the intelligence and charm of their larger counterparts.

10-15 inches 15-17 lbs Limited

Toy Poodle

The tiny but mighty Toy Poodle stands under 10 inches tall. Despite their small size, they are incredibly intelligent, agile, and make perfect companions for apartment living.

Under 10 inches 6-9 lbs Reservations
